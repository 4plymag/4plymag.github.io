4PLY is a small, independent 'magazine' taking a data-driven approach to skateboarding content.



Who?

The official 4PLY team consists of a Data Scientist in California, a Marketing Analyst in Oregon, and a hacker in Australia. We get lots of help and contributions from other cool people. Check each article to see who participated.

Why?

To (hopefully) provide skateboarders with interesting content in a novel way and maybe learn some coding and data skills while we're at it. Think some combination between Jenkem Mag and FiveThirtyEight. Also the pandemic shutdown was super boring.

Is it 'four-ply' or 'foreplay'?

Most skateboards have seven layers of maple wood (7ply). Using only 4ply implies lower quality. 'Foreplay' is cool too, though apparently an area we "don't know much about" (whatever that means, Elizabeth).

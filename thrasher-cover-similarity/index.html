What Is This?

Explore 506 Thrasher Magazine covers from 1981 to 2021, arranged by visual similarity using machine learning. (AI, so hot right now!). Basically, Thrasher covers that are closer to each other are more similar, either in terms of content or color.

By Content

Covers are positioned using CLIP embeddings, a neural network that understands image content. Similar covers cluster together: transition shots, title cards, similar backgrounds, portraits, arm positions, etc. naturally form groups.

By Color

Covers are arranged by their color palette.

Controls

Click and drag, scroll or pinch to pan and zoom. Hover over any cover to see relevant details.

Filter by decade, skater, trick, obstacle, and search across all metadata.
